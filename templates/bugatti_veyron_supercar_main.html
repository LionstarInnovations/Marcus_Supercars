Bugatti Veyron

For those with all the money in the world and a desire to go blisteringly fast, there’s the Bugatti Veyron 16.4. It’s a carbon-fiber and aluminum masterpiece with an 8.0-liter turbo-quad W16 engine, seven-speed automatic and all-wheel drive; it makes 1001 hp and has a 200-mph-plus top speed. For wind in your hair, try the Grand Sport with its targa top. For even more speed, the 1200-hp Super Sport and Grand Sport Vitesse are sure to please.
